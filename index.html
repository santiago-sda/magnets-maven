The Practice

What is
Biomagnetism?

Biomagnetism is a simple, gentle, yet powerful therapy that detects imbalances in the body due to germs, toxins, or emotions. The practitioner places medium-sized magnets on specific points of the body using different polarities to clear these germs, toxins, imbalances, or emotions.

By applying magnetic pairs to specific points on the body, we help restore your natural pH. When the pH is balanced (neutral) your body starts to heal naturally.

Biomagnetism is a complementary practice and is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any medical condition. Please consult your healthcare provider for any health concerns.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What should I wear?

Comfortable, loose-fitting clothing is ideal — something you can rest in easily. You remain fully clothed throughout the entire session, so there is no need to change or undress.

Is the session painful?

No. Biomagnetic therapy is a gentle, non-invasive practice. Magnets are placed on the body with care — there is no pressure, manipulation, or discomfort involved. Most clients find the experience deeply relaxing.

How long does a session last?

Sessions typically last between 60 and 75 minutes. The introductory session may take a little longer, as it includes a brief intake conversation so we can get to know your needs before we begin.

Is biomagnetism a replacement for medical care?

No. Biomagnetism is a complementary wellness practice and should not be used as a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. If you have a health concern, please consult your doctor or healthcare provider. Biomagnetic therapy is intended to complement — not replace — conventional care.

Can I book if I am pregnant, have a pacemaker, or have implanted medical devices?

If you are pregnant, have a pacemaker, or have any implanted medical devices (such as cochlear implants or insulin pumps), please reach out before booking. We ask that you share this information so we can discuss whether a session is appropriate for you. Your safety is always the priority.

What should I expect after the session?

Many clients feel a sense of calm and lightness after a session. We recommend drinking plenty of water, resting if you feel the need to, and allowing yourself a gentle, easy evening. Some people notice a shift over the following day or two — simply be attentive to how your body responds.
